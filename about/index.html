About Shawe

Hey there! I'm David Shawe, a designer and solo-founder from Northern Ireland. I run a business called Invisible Building. Invisible Building is a design lead company and we specialise in User experience, designing and building digital prototypes, launching no-code MVPs and helping startups use open-source ai to their advantage. We do this and more for a diverse range of clients. Invisible Building focus on three main pillars of work: e-commerce, startups, and tech for good.

In addition to my work at Invisible Building, I also ventured into fintech research by founding Sugar Financial Technology, this led me to explore Bitcoin from a designer's perspective. My experience took me through the Catalyst Co-Founders programme and a Royal Academy of Engineering programme, where I developed a prototype tip jar using Bitcoin technology.

I'm also dedicated to my local community, and I co-founded Moira Community, an association aimed at fostering collaboration, innovation, and positive change among like-minded individuals for the growth and development of my hometown. Through this project, we're creating a platform for collective growth and improvement that will benefit everyone in the Moira area and beyond.

Throughout my career, I've honed my skills in automation, design, code, and no-code projects, working with various companies and teams to bring their vision to life. I'm always eager to take on new challenges, whether it's mentoring young talents through CoderDojo or working on cutting-edge projects that push the boundaries of what's possible.

As a designer who loves the energy of startups, I'm dedicated to making a positive impact through technology and design. If you're interested in connecting with a like-minded individual who is passionate about supporting the growth of the Northern Irish startup ecosystem and building tech for good services, I'd love to hear from you!
